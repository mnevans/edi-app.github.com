ABOUT EDI



Who is Edi?
Edi, born in January 2018, provides first-class assistance to people who need it. Whether that's having an errand run, catching a ride, needing a sitter, Edi is there.



How do I use Edi?
You can ask Edi for help right here, or by downloading the mobile application.



Where can I find Edi?
