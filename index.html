ABOUT EDI



Who is Edi?
Edi, born in January 2018, provides first-class assistance to people who need it. Whether that's running an errand, managing a busy schedule, finding a sitter—Edi is there.



How do I use Edi?
You can ask Edi for help right here, or by downloading the mobile application.



Where can I find Edi?
Currently Edi is only in the Greater Seattle Area, but would love to travel more and assist people all over.
